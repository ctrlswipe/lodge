A History Carved in Wood

Established in 1924 as a hunter's refuge, The Owl Lodge has stood silent witness to a century of Norwegian winters. In 2023, it was meticulously restored by award-winning architects, blending the original timber soul with modern brutalist elegance.

Located in the heart of Telemark, we are surrounded by whispering pines and historical echoes. Accessibility is an adventure in itself—reachable by a private 4x4 track in summer, or a guided snowmobile transfer in winter. We define luxury not by excess, but by the profound connection to nature.
